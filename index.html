9 Reasons Why Rabbits Make Good Pets!!

Rabbits help you get healthy.

Rabbits help you get healthy as herbivores, rabbits mesh well with vegetarian and vegan lifestyles. Generally speaking, though, having a pet who encourages you to stock your fridge with fruits and vegetables is good for everybody â€” vegetarians and meat-eaters alike.

They provide better companionship

Having a rabbit as a pet is perfect for those who may not have time for daily walks, but still seek the social quality of a dog-like companion. And, like cats, rabbits can be litter box trained very easily. Best of both worlds!

Rabbits live long life

Rabbits have long lifespans compared to other small animals. They can live 10-12 years if provided with a proper diet and care.

Rabbis are heroic

Rabbits are heroic. In the wild, rabbits communicate with each other about perceived dangers by thumping their back legs; astute house bunnies will provide you with a similar security system.

Rabbits are quiet

If you live in an apartment building or have nearby neighbors, animal sounds can be a big concern.Rabbits male little or no noise as they are typically very quiet - no barking, whining, meowing, etc. that could upset housemates, neighbors, and landlords! plus hey do not require a lot of space

Rabbits can purr when contended

Contrary to dogs or particularly vocal cats, rabbits are very quiet animals. They make the occasional sound, but by and large, they're not much for making noise

Rabbits form strong bonds

Rabbits are intelligent and emotionally complex creatures. Rabbits are very loving, social animals, which means they not only love to spend time with their humans they require it.

They are always fun

Rabbits are uniquely talented comedians. Bunnies do somewhat of (little hop-spins and kicks they do when they're happy), bunny flops (flopping over and playing dead) are just some of the quirky and amusing habits of the house bunny.

Rabbits are great listeners

Merely looking at them (just look at those ears!). They also make excellent snugglers with their extra-soft fur and loving nature. What more could you ask for from a friend?
